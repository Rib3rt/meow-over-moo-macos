## About This Game

Meow Over Moo is a 1v1 turn-based tactics boardgame where cute factions clash in a battle of positioning, timing, and smart trades.

Command your forces on a battlefield grid, manage your supply, protect your Commandant, and exploit every tactical opening. Win by destroying the enemy Commandant or eliminating every enemy unit.

Play your way:

- Challenge AI opponents in single player
- Face a friend in local multiplayer
- Battle online and climb the global rating leaderboard

### Key Features

- 1v1 turn-based tactical battles
- Boardgame-inspired battlefield play
- Single player with AI opponents
- Local multiplayer
- Online multiplayer
- Global rating leaderboard
- Full controller support
- Steam Deck-friendly controls
- In-game Unit Codex for quick unit reference

### Win Conditions

- Destroy the enemy Commandant
- Or eliminate all enemy units

Simple to understand, difficult to master.
